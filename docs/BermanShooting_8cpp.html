Detailed Description

Solving the fourth-order Berman (porous channel) similarity equation

where and is a pressure constant and is the Reynolds number based on the channel half-height and the wall suction. The boundary conditions are that and . This BVP is solved via Runge-Kutta and (vector) Newton iteration. This similarity form is an exact reduction of the Navier-Stokes equations.

Solving a BVP by converting it to an IVP like this is generally not a smart move!
